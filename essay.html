My last winter holiday was usual. I stayed at home all the time! Except Sometimes I played in the garden downstairs.

However the days at home wasn't too boring. I studied programming and Front-End courses. I wrote many webpages by using those knowledge that time. I learned a lot on Bilibili website.

In the rest of the time, I often play The Hive the Minecraft server. I had a great time.
