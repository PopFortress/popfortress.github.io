A Countryball's Family

荣获“抽象”评价、14/15分的小作文
Hello guys I'm the USA, the countryball who wears sunglasses. Here's a photo of my family.
I'm the small ball in the middle, just between my parents. My father the UK is on the left side. He was the best, he gave me a lot of lands and money. My mother France is on the right side. She is kind to me, she likes revolution and many kinds of arts. My cousin Europe Union hides in the right corner. He and my father's relationship is complex. He looks scared in the photo, perhaps he dreamed about something scary.
This is my family, I love all balls in my family.
